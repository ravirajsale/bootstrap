Amazing Ziplines

This is the perfect activity for people who enjoy high adrenaline experiences. This adventurous zipline is comprised of 11 platforms of varying height. From your unique vantage point you will be able to see colorful landscapes swish by below you as you cross over the Tizate River. You can reach the starting point of this tour via horseback or in a van.

Revitalizing Mud Therapy

Guests at Vandará will find a mineral rich mud basin for body treatments. This all natural mud is extracted from the Rincón de la Vieja volcano and thus, rich in minerals that are good for the body and health. This mud therapy has benefits such as exfoliation and softening of the skin, an improvement in circulation, and the alleviation of some skin conditions.
